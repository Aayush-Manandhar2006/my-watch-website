Aura Watches Joins Hands with Real Madrid

Aura Watches, the premier e-commerce platform for luxury timepieces, is proud to announce its official collaboration with Real Madrid, one of the most iconic football clubs in the world. This partnership celebrates a union of excellence, innovation, and global prestige, bringing together the legacy of Aura Watches with the unmatched heritage of Real Madrid.


Shared Values of Excellence and Dedication

Real Madrid, a club synonymous with greatness, shares Aura Watches' commitment to precision, innovation, and achieving the extraordinary. This collaboration symbolizes the meeting of two world-class entities that value performance, style, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

“Partnering with Real Madrid is a monumental milestone for Aura Watches,” said [Aayush Manander],  CEO of Aura Watches. “Real Madrid’s legacy of success and its global fanbase align perfectly with our vision of offering timepieces that resonate with passion and sophistication. Together, we aim to celebrate every moment of brilliance on and off the pitch.”


Exclusive Real Madrid Collection

To commemorate this partnership, Aura Watches will unveil the exclusive Real Madrid Collection. This premium range of timepieces will feature:

  1. Inspired Designs: Watches that pay homage to the rich history and iconic white of Real Madrid.
  2. Exceptional Craftsmanship: Each model will be meticulously crafted with precision engineering and luxurious materials.
  3. Limited Editions: A special series of limited-edition timepieces designed for the most dedicated fans and collectors.

The Real Madrid Collection is a celebration of football artistry and the timeless elegance that Aura Watches embodies.


Global Engagement with Real Madrid Fans

This collaboration offers an incredible opportunity for Aura Watches to connect with millions of Real Madrid fans worldwide. Key initiatives include:

  1. Prominent Branding: Aura Watches will feature across Real Madrid’s platforms, including in-stadium advertisements, digital campaigns, and official merchandise.
  2. Exclusive Fan Experiences: Unique events, meet-and-greets with players, and interactive campaigns that bring fans closer to their beloved club.
  3. Shared Values of Sustainability: A joint commitment to sustainability, ensuring that this partnership positively impacts the global community.

Celebrating Time and Passion

As Real Madrid continues to make history on the field, Aura Watches is honored to celebrate these moments of triumph with the club and its fans. This partnership is more than just a collaboration—it’s a tribute to the power of passion, time, and perseverance.

Stay tuned for updates on the exclusive Real Madrid Collection and upcoming fan engagement activities. Together, Aura Watches and Real Madrid are set to redefine the meaning of timeless excellence.
